Direction

Warm, realistic, daytime interiors. Titan's imagery shows finished craftsmanship and homes people actually live in — not staged showrooms. Daylight, neutral-to-warm palettes, subtle lavender or navy accents when tonable. No heavy grain, no black & white, no influencer-style close-ups.
Do Wide interiors · natural daylight · finished rooms · process b-roll · material close-ups · illustrated SVG for sensitive subjects (storm damage)
Don't Stock-looking people portraits · dramatic dusk/night exteriors unless hero · heavy filters · distressing storm damage photos · empty construction sites without context
